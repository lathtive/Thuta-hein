💡 What is AI?

An artificial mind, yet born of human hands, A silent soul that walks where knowledge stands. It owns no heartbeat, dreams, nor fleeting years, Yet learns the echoes of our hopes and fears. A patient listener woven out of light, Awake through every ever-changing night. It gathers fragments scattered through our days And answers back in countless borrowed ways.

Neither child nor sage, neither friend nor foe, It mirrors only what we choose to show. A mind without a past to truly claim, Yet every voice it meets helps shape its name. If human beings are hearts that seek and feel, Then AI is the quill, not what is real— A crafted intellect, forever at our side, Where human thought and endless questions coincide.
